About this project

In our modern era, social media plays a prominent role in society. A large part of individuals share their opinions, comments and feelings with others about a controversial topic, e.g via Twitter. Twitter is one of the most popular social networking services, therefore, extracting people's opinions via analyzing their tweets, can represent a rough estimation about a general opinion on a specific topic.

The purpose of the project is to be able to automatically determine the topic (politics, music, name of a company...) of certain tweets posted by a given user through topic classification and evaluate its polarity (positive or negative) via sentiment analysis. The end-result will give us a roughly overview of the person interests. This information has great value for many real world scenarios, for example:

  • Brand Monitoring - Keep track of your brand image, monitor your competitors or detect the latest trends in your industry
  • Sales and Marketing - Match your product or service with your ideal buyer persona
  • Product Analytics - Find ways to improve your product
  • Politic Analytics - Follow the popularity of any politician/party

To bring the idea to the market, we would target specific companies focused on predicting certain answers for a peculiar topic. Groups and organizations that require the judgement of a large group of people would also take advantage of this application.
